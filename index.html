Positive outcomes

  • We enable to assist grade 12 learners to be admitted at higher education institutions
  • We encouraged learners to apply for Learnerships with the aim of acquiring skills
  • We assisted youth with making of identity document books or cards
  • We empowered youth with the literacy of life skills
  • We assisted youth with managerial skills to establish their own businesses with the aim to encourage job makers instead of job seekers
  • We also assist unemployment graduate to find internship and request them to volunteer in the organisation with the intention of gaining experience
